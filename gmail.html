Gmail sign out issue


A design improvement for gmail to easily logout of a single account.





Context:



We all use Gmail quite a lot. And sometimes we have multiple accounts logged in at the same time - college, business, work, personal. Likewise, a while back, I was trying to log out of one of my accounts when I realised that such an option is not available on Gmail. You can either log out of all accounts or not log out at all.




Problem:


  • Not being able to log out of a single account at a time.
  • Compulsorily having to log out of all accounts, every time.










Suggestion:


  • Give the option to sign out of every single account, individually.
  • Keep the 'sign out of all accounts' button as well.
  • Designed in Figma.
